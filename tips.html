writing the email

  • Emails can be blocked by keyword, so please consider changing your email instead of using the same templates! Making the email unique and adding your own message will make it harder for them to mass-block.

  • Be creative with your message! This can include spelling Gregory Johnson's name differently, like "Gr3g0ry J0hns0n" for example. Also can include changing the subject line.

  • There are email templates available on here if you need examples.

  • For email templates of contacting elected officials, use this

  • Keep sending emails EVERYDAY. Whatever it takes, for as long as it takes.
